TechSmith Camtasia User Experience Research and Design

TechSmith is a software company that owns Camtasia, a video creation platform. I worked with TechSmith clients to develop a solution to the problem that novice Camtasia users have difficulty completing a video project. We identified that the main problem is that users lack discoverability of the available tools and are unaware of editing functions. As a result, we developed a hi-fi prototype for a help wizard.
